# OmniSight-AI

> AI-Powered Income Protection Platform for India’s Gig Delivery Workforce

---
## Problem Statement

India’s gig delivery ecosystem powers millions of daily transactions across food, grocery, and e-commerce platforms. However, delivery partners often face income disruptions caused by uncontrollable external events such as:

- Extreme weather conditions (heavy rain, heatwaves, storms)
- Natural disasters
- High pollution levels
- Government curfews or social disruptions

During such events, deliveries decline or stop entirely, leaving delivery partners with zero income despite being available to work.

Most traditional insurance products do not cover short-term income loss caused by environmental or social disruptions, leaving gig workers financially vulnerable.

---
## Target Persona

OmniSight AI focuses on food delivery partners in India, particularly those working with platforms such as:

- Zomato
- Swiggy
- Amazon

Typical characteristics of the target user:

- Works 5–6 days per week
- Earnings depend entirely on completed deliveries
- Paid on a weekly payout cycle
- Highly vulnerable to disruptions outside their control
